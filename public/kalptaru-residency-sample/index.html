About the Residency

A Sanctuary of Calm & Culture

Tucked beside the bustling Pari Chowk and India Expo Centre, KalpTaru Residency is a boutique retreat designed for travellers who appreciate quiet elegance. Nine thoughtfully appointed suites, each with a private balcony, open onto a verdant garden anchored by a traditional temple — a small piece of heritage in the middle of the city.

Whether you're here for business at the Expo Mart, a wedding at the banquet, or a peaceful weekend escape, every detail — from the in-room kitchenette to the 24-hour front desk — is shaped to make you feel at home.
