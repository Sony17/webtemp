Questions

Frequently asked questions.

Tap a question to expand the answer.

How long does installation take?
Most wallpaper rooms are installed in 4–8 hours. WPC louver walls and PVC panels typically take 1–2 days depending on area. We bring all materials and tools — no mess left behind.
Do you do free site visits?
Yes. For Firozabad and nearby areas, our team can come measure, sample shades, and recommend products at no charge. WhatsApp 097600 17505 to schedule.
What's the warranty on wallpapers and panels?
Wallpapers carry a 5–8 year colour and peel warranty depending on brand. PVC and WPC panels are warranted for 10+ years against warping and moisture damage.
Can I get custom prints or sizes?
Absolutely. We do custom-print blinds (photos, religious imagery), custom-size wallpaper rolls, and made-to-order sofas in fabric and colour of your choice.
What's the price range?
Wallpaper from ₹40/sq ft (custom UV print ₹70/sq ft). Zebra blinds ₹200/sq ft. 3D PVC tile ₹200/pc. 17mm WPC panel ₹400/pc. Charcoal panel ₹550/pc. Sofas are custom-quoted.
Do you deliver outside Firozabad?
Yes — we deliver across Uttar Pradesh and arrange transport to neighbouring states for larger orders.
